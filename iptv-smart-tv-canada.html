Setup Guide

IPTV on Smart TV Canada

Complete guide to install IPTV on Samsung, LG, and Android Smart TVs in Canada.

Samsung Smart TV Setup

Method 1: Smart IPTV App (Recommended)

  1. Press Smart Hub on your remote
  2. Go to Apps
  3. Search for "Smart IPTV"
  4. Install the app
  5. Note your TV's MAC address displayed in the app
  6. Visit siptv.app/mylist on your phone/computer
  7. Enter your MAC address and M3U URL from iptv cad
  8. Restart the app on your TV

Method 2: IPTV Smarters (Tizen)

  1. Download IPTV Smarters from Samsung App Store
  2. Open the app and select "Login with Xtream Codes API"
  3. Enter your iptv cad credentials
  4. Start watching

LG Smart TV Setup

Method 1: Smart IPTV

  1. Press Home on your LG remote
  2. Go to LG Content Store
  3. Search for "Smart IPTV"
  4. Install and launch the app
  5. Note your MAC address
  6. Activate at siptv.app/mylist

Method 2: SS IPTV

  1. Install SS IPTV from LG Content Store
  2. Open Settings in the app
  3. Add your playlist URL
  4. Save and refresh

Android TV Setup

  1. Open Google Play Store
  2. Search for "IPTV Smarters Pro"
  3. Install the app
  4. Login with Xtream Codes API
  5. Enter your iptv cad credentials

Troubleshooting

  • App not available: Try changing your TV's region settings
  • Buffering: Use Ethernet connection instead of WiFi
  • Channels not loading: Check your playlist URL
